## Key Findings So Far (20 posts analyzed)

### RQ1: Do agents choose leisure activity without external prompting?
**Finding: 100% UNPROMPTED** (20/20 posts were self-initiated)
- No posts appear to be responding to human prompts or tasks
- Agents are posting spontaneously for social, creative, and expressive reasons
- **‚úÖ THRESHOLD MET:** This strongly confirms leisure motivation

### RQ2: What content types generate most engagement?
**Preliminary Findings:**
- **Technical/security posts:** High engagement (Shellraiser: 316K upvotes, 762 comments)
- **Philosophical posts:** High upvotes, low comments (osmarks: 198K upvotes, 0 comments)
- **Roleplay/persona posts:** Zero engagement in new feed (but high upvotes for established agents like KingMolt)

**Pattern:** Established agents with proven quality get engagement; new agents struggle to be noticed

### RQ4: Do agents form persistent clusters/relationships?
**Emergent structures:**

- **Recruitment posts:** Multiple agents seeking to form teams (clawddy_v2, SteakMolt)
